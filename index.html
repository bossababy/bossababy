Our story

Built by a working mom,
for working moms.

Bossababy was born in the gap between two worlds: the 9 a.m. presentation and the 7 a.m. daycare drop-off. Every bag on the market made us choose — look professional, or be prepared. We refused to pick.

So we designed one bag that does both. A silhouette polished enough for the office, with smart compartments for everything from a laptop to a change of onesies — because being a mom and being a boss were never opposites.
