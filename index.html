Our story

Built by a working mom,
for working moms.

Bossababy was born in the gap between two worlds. Every bag on the market made us choose: look polished, or be prepared. We refused to pick.

So we designed one bag that does both. A silhouette refined enough for the boardroom, engineered with intelligent compartments for everything from a laptop to a change of onesies — because motherhood and elegance were never opposites.
